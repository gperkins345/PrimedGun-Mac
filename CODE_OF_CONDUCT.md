# PrimedGun Code of Conduct

PrimedGun is a collaborative open source project. Contributors, testers, and
users are expected to keep discussion respectful, practical, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent where reasonable, but correct problems clearly.
- Respect different experience levels, platforms, hardware, and play styles.
- Follow project legal rules around licensing, clean-source work, and game data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Posting or threatening to post private information.
- Discriminatory jokes or language.
- Sexualized, violent, or otherwise inappropriate material in project spaces.
- Repeatedly pushing a discussion after being asked to stop.
- Sharing or requesting pirated games, copyrighted game assets, BIOS files,
  keys, proprietary SDK files, or leaked/confidential material.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, moderate, block, or remove people from project spaces when
needed to protect the project and its contributors. Serious or repeated issues
may result in a permanent ban from project spaces.

Report issues to the project maintainers through GitHub or the active project
community channels.
